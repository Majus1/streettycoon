StreetTycoon

Massive Multiplayer Online Incremental game

Welcome to StreetTycoon! Buy streets and buildings, earn rent, and expand your empire. Trade, sabotage, and bid in real-time auctions to dominate the city. Get ready for a thrilling journey to financial success!

Building Your Empire

Acquire, Expand, Compete, and Dominate

Buy real life streets and buildings and start earning rent. Increase your empire and collect rewarding bonuses. Sabotage other users or trade with them. Take part in real time auctions and be the highest bidder.

Rise to the Top

Strategize, Compete, and Conquer in the Urban Arena

Every player begins on equal footing with one shared objective to become the StreetTycoon. Acquire streets and buildings to generate rent, build your empire.

Expand and Prosper

Unlock Bonuses and Achieve Tycoon Status

As your empire expands, gain bonuses by buying intersecting streets. Reach a certain net worth to unlock the Tycoon Bonus, boosting all rent payouts.

Sabotage & Negotiate

Outwit Rivals and Forge Alliances

Someone bought your favorite street? You can build a sabotaging factory and it’ll stop earning them rent. You can also send them an offer.

Bid to Win

Auctions for Victory! Outbid, Outsmart, and Conquer

Having secured, say, Wall Street as your initial purchase, you now have the opportunity to auction it off. If there's demand, you stand to make a profitable sale.
